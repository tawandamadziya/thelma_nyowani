About Me
Learn a bit about MC_Afro

My name is Thelma Nyowani(Creator of MC_AFRO). I am based in Cape Town, South Africa. The best way to describe myself is Im ambitious. I believe in creativity. Creativity comes from all walks of life and that doesnt stop me from helping to uplift those who deserve it most.

I believe in creativity and im a lover of art in social media. I love creating influence in my circle as well as outside of my usual groups. My goal in this life is to leave a legacy, a positive impact in every industry known to social media thus I have started filming my journey as an MC(master of ceremonies), fashion model, talkshow host and social media influencer.

At the end of the day only one thing matters and that is creating a positive change and uplifting many youths as well as modernising the world of hosting and social medial
